Abbreviations in Latin Inscriptions Beginning with the Letter Z

  

Compiled by Tom Elliott (tom_elliott@unc.edu)
for the ASGLE web site

 

Z Z(erna), z(eses), Z(osimi), Z(ucchabar)
ZAM Zam(a), Zam(ensium)
ZB Zb(elsurdo)
ZEG Ze(u)g(mae)
ZEGVSLM Ze(u)g(mae) v(otum) s(olvit) l(ibens) m(erito)
ZEL Zel(oti)
ZELO Zelo(ti)
ZENO Zeno(ne)
ZENOCOSCOH Zeno(ne) co(n)s(ulibus) coh(ortis)
ZEP Zep(hyros)
ZES zes(es)
ZETH Zeth(i), Zeth(us)
ZETZI Zetzi(s)
ZETZICONIUX Zetzi(s) coniu(n)x
ZIS zi(e)s
ZOIL Zoil(i)
ZOSI Zosi(mus)
ZOSIM Zosim(i)
ZOSIME Zosim(a)e
